What is User Interface and User Experience Design?

According to Don Norman, "'User experience' encompasses all aspects of the end-user's interaction with the company, its services, and its products." Don Norman, a cognitive scientist, was the VP of the Advanced Technology Group at Apple in the 90's UX designers typically focus on the progression and growth of digital products. This can be anything from a web identity to a web site and even extend to mobile applications.
